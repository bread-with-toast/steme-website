Multimeters

  • Multimeters are electronic gadgets which can be used to debug electronic circuits.
  • Multimeters come in two forms; analog multimeters and digital multimeters.
  • Analog multimeters have a display which points at the value the multimeter is currently at.
  • On the other hand, digital multimeters have a digital display which shows the value of the number.
  • Multimeters have two probes, a red probe (to VCC) and a black probe (to GND).
  • To measure the value of something with a multimeter, make sure you are at the correct unit.
