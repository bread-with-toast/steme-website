Resistors & Capacitors

  • Resistors are used in electronics to protect components.
  • Resistors are measured in ohms
  • Resistors are not polarised, which means they can be placed in any way
  • You can measure the value of a resistor using a multimeter or an ohmeter
  • Capacitors can hold and release charge quickly
  • Electrolytic capacitors are polarised, and can hold more charge than a ceramic capacitor.
  • Ceramic capacitors are not polarised
  • You can measure a capacitor with a multimeter with Farads
  • Capacitors can be used to make sure that voltage is spread evenly across a circuit.
